How VaultPilot works

Users & Strategies

Gemini AI analyzes risk tolerance and market conditions to determine optimal, dynamic strategies.

  1. User deposits funds and defines risk tolerance via the VaultPilot Dashboard.
  2. Google Gemini AI analyzes market volatility, liquidity, and risk in real-time.
  3. AI-optimized strategies are pushed to the core immutable smart contract.
  4. The smart contract automatically executes rebalancing across DeFi protocols (like Aave or Uniswap).
  5. Dashboard provides a real-time view of current allocation and system activity.
Verifiable on-chain

VaultPilot logic executes via immutable smart contracts, making all actions verifiable.

Adaptive AI

The Gemini AI brain integrates directly, enabling continuous strategy updates without changing core contracts.

AI-Driven Logic

Strategy is powered by an LLM model, not fixed rule sets, for intelligent, automated rebalancing.
